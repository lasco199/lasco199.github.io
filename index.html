Who We Are

Argo Park Kayak Club is a group of whitewater kayaking enthusiasts in southeastern Michigan. While there are no high grade whitewater rivers in this part of the state, we still need to keep our skills sharp and make new friends! We typically gather on weekends at Argo Cascades ("Argo Park") near Ann Arbor, but frequently organize trips to other play spots or rivers in Michigan and sometimes multi-day trips out of state. Check us out on Facebook!

Get Started

We emphasize safety as the most important when you're getting started with whitewater. You need a helmet, and a life-jacket (or PFD) to be safe out there on the water. You also need a whitewater kayak appropriate for your skill level and the conditions you'll be paddling in, and a spray skirt that fits you and your boat.

What Next?

Feel free to come on out to Argo Cascades on Sunday afternoons to join in on the fun! If you're already familiar with Argo Park, or you want to take your skills to the next level, hop on one of our trips down the Huron River! And if you're already beyond that, check out our trips to Slippery Rock Creek or the Youghiogheny River in PA! There's something for everyone out here!
